Welcome to RedPoint!

RedPoint is a mockup bouldering gym. It does not exist.
However, this website aims to portray a realistic user experience. It offers interactive features such as keeping track of your completed routes and rating boulders.

Our boulders are set by our experienced local setting team and at least one guest setter.
You will notice how there is a wide variety of styles, but a familiar vibe throughout all of them.
